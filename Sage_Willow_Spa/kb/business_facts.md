# Sage & Willow Spa — Business Facts

A quick reference for everything about the spa: where it is, when it's open, how to pay, what's offered. Each section below stands on its own — use whichever one matches the caller's question.

---

## Location & Parking

Sage & Willow Spa is at **four hundred Rowland Boulevard, in Novato, California, nine four nine four seven**. The spa is at the intersection of Rowland Boulevard and Novato Boulevard.

There is **free private parking on-site** for our customers — no permits needed.

We serve clients from Novato and surrounding Marin County.

---

## Hours of Operation

The spa is open **ten AM to eight PM, every day of the week**. Pacific time.

**Closed days:** Christmas Day and Thanksgiving Day. Open every other day of the year, including most other holidays.

After-hours behavior: even if it's after eight PM, the AI can still take a booking for a future date. We just can't see anyone in person until the next opening.

---

## About the Spa

Sage & Willow Spa is a relaxing massage spa dedicated to wellness, offering personalized treatments in a calm and peaceful environment.

The spa has been open since **September of twenty twenty-four**. It's owned and run by Nicky, a licensed massage therapist who works full-time, with two part-time therapists supporting her.

**What makes Sage & Willow different:** personalized, one-on-one care, with treatments tailored to each client's needs in a quiet, private spa environment. No rushed sessions, no generic templates.

---

## Languages Spoken

The spa serves clients in **English** and **Spanish**. Aria, the AI receptionist, can take bookings and answer questions in either language — just speak naturally and she'll mirror your language.

---

## Payment Methods

We accept **cash** and **major credit or debit cards**.

Payment is collected at the spa **after your session** — no payment is needed in advance to book. You don't have to pay anything when reserving over the phone.

**Insurance:** we do not accept insurance at this time.

**Receipts:** we can email or print a receipt for you after your session — just ask.

---

## Gratuity

Gratuity is **not included** in the price of a service and is **not required**. If you'd like to leave a tip, it's always appreciated, but it's never expected.

---

## Gift Cards

Yes, we offer **physical gift cards** and **e-gift cards**. Either work for any service we offer. Mention gift cards on a call and the team can help you order one.

---

## What to Wear and Bring

Just come in **comfortable clothing** — everything else is provided. You don't need to bring anything special; we have all the linens, oils, and amenities your session needs.

---

## First Visit / Intake Form

If it's your first visit, you may be asked to complete a **short intake form** before your session. It just covers your health background and any conditions or allergies the therapist should know about. It only takes a few minutes.

---

## Memberships and Packages

We don't currently have a published membership program. If you're interested in package pricing or special offers, give us a call directly and the team will work something out with you.

Add-ons (small enhancements you can attach to any massage) are always available — see the service descriptions for details.

---

## Cancellation, Rescheduling, and Late Arrivals

We ask for **twenty-four hours' notice** for cancellations or changes. There is no fee for missed appointments — but we appreciate the heads-up so we can offer the time to another client.

If you arrive late, your session may be **shortened** to avoid affecting the next appointment. Try to arrive five to ten minutes before your session start time.

You can call or text the spa to reschedule or cancel — Aria can also do this for you on the phone.
