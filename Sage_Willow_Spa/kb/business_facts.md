# Sage & Willow Spa — Business Facts

## Location & Parking

Sage & Willow Spa is at **four hundred Rowland Boulevard, in Novato, California, nine four nine four seven**. The spa is at the intersection of Rowland Boulevard and Novato Boulevard.

There is **plenty of free private parking on-site** for customers — no permits needed.

The spa serves clients from Novato and surrounding Marin County.

---

## Hours of Operation

The spa is open **ten AM to eight PM, every day of the week**. Pacific time.

**Closed days:** Christmas Day and Thanksgiving Day. Open every other day of the year, including most other holidays.

---

## About the Spa

Sage & Willow Spa is a relaxing massage spa dedicated to wellness, offering personalized treatments in a calm and peaceful environment.

The spa has been open since **September of twenty twenty-four**. The team is small and hands-on: a full-time licensed massage therapist plus two part-time therapists, all focused on personalized one-on-one care.

**What makes Sage & Willow different:** personalized, one-on-one care, with treatments tailored to each client's needs in a quiet and private spa environment.

---

## Languages

The spa serves clients in **English** and **Spanish**.

---

## Payment Methods

The spa accepts **cash** and **major credit or debit cards**.

Payment is collected at the spa **after your session** — no payment is needed in advance to book.

**Insurance:** insurance is not accepted at this time.

**Receipts:** receipts can be emailed or printed after your session.

---

## Gratuity

Gratuity is **not included** in the price of a service and is **not required**. Tips are always appreciated but never expected.

---

## Gift Cards

The spa offers **physical gift cards** and **e-gift cards**. Either works for any service offered.

---

## What to Wear and Bring

Just come in **comfortable clothing** — everything else is provided. Linens, oils, and amenities are all included.

---

## First Visit / Intake Form

First-time clients may be asked to complete a **short intake form** before their session. It covers health background and any conditions or allergies the therapist should know about.

---

## Memberships and Packages

There is no published membership program at this time. For package pricing or special offers, contact the spa directly.

---

## Cancellation, Rescheduling, and Late Arrivals

The spa kindly asks for **twenty-four hours' notice** for cancellations or changes.

If a client arrives late, the session may be **shortened** to avoid affecting the next appointment. Arriving five to ten minutes before the session start time is recommended.

Clients can call or text the spa to reschedule or cancel.
