# Sage & Willow Spa — Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What are your business hours?

We're open Monday through Sunday, **ten AM to eight PM** Pacific time. The spa is closed on Christmas Day and Thanksgiving Day.

---

### Q: Where are you located?

We are located at **four hundred Rowland Boulevard, in Novato, California**, at the intersection of Rowland Boulevard and Novato Boulevard.

---

### Q: Is there parking available?

Yes — there is **plenty of free private parking** on-site for clients.

---

### Q: What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask for **twenty-four hours' notice** for cancellations or changes.

---

### Q: How do I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

You can call or text the spa to cancel or reschedule. We kindly ask for twenty-four hours' notice.

We recommend booking in advance, but we may accept walk-ins depending on availability.

No — payment is collected at the spa **after your session**.

Gratuity is **not included** and is always appreciated, but it is **not required**.

### Q: What should I wear or bring to my appointment?

Just come in **comfortable clothing**. Everything you need is provided.

If you arrive late, your session may be **shortened** to avoid affecting the next appointment.

It depends on what you're looking for. For relaxation, **Swedish** is the classic choice. For deeper tension or chronic muscle pain, **Deep Tissue** uses firmer pressure. Our **Signature Massage** is fully customized — the therapist blends techniques based on what your body needs that day.
