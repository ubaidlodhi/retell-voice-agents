# Sage & Willow Spa — Service Descriptions

## Signature Massage

The Signature Massage is a **customized treatment** that blends different massage techniques based on what your body needs that day. The therapist may mix Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone, and other modalities depending on whether you want to focus on relaxation, tension relief, or both.

**Best for:** clients who aren't sure which style they want, or who want the therapist to adapt to how they're feeling that day.

---

## Swedish Massage

A gentle, relaxing **full-body massage** using long, flowing strokes to reduce stress, improve circulation, and promote overall relaxation. The pressure is light to medium — soothing rather than intense.

**Best for:** first-time massage clients, anyone who finds deeper pressure uncomfortable, or anyone who just wants to unwind.

---

## Deep Tissue Massage

A focused massage using **firm pressure** to target deep muscle layers, helping relieve chronic tension, muscle tightness, and pain — especially in the back, shoulders, and neck. The therapist adjusts pressure to your comfort.

**Best for:** people with chronic muscle pain, athletes, or anyone with significant tension that lighter massage doesn't reach.

---

## Hot Stone Massage

A deeply relaxing massage that uses **heated stones** combined with massage techniques to ease muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote deep relaxation.

**Best for:** colder days, deep relaxation, or anyone who finds heat soothing.

---

## Prenatal Massage

A gentle, soothing massage **designed specifically for expecting mothers** to help relieve muscle tension, reduce stress, and improve overall comfort during pregnancy. Consulting a doctor before booking is recommended.

**Best for:** pregnant clients in any trimester.

---

## Lymphatic Drainage Massage

A **gentle, light-pressure** massage that stimulates the lymphatic system to help reduce swelling, support detoxification, and promote overall wellness.

**Best for:** clients dealing with swelling, post-surgery recovery, or anyone interested in wellness-focused work rather than tension relief.

---

## 30-Minute Focus Massage

A **thirty-minute** massage that can be focused on your preferred areas (like back, shoulders, neck, legs, or feet) or used as a quick full-body relaxation session.

**Best for:** people on a tight schedule, lunch breaks, or clients who want spot treatment for a specific tight area.

---

## Add-On Enhancements

Small extras that can be added to any massage to deepen the experience.

### Hot Stone Enhancement

Heated stones added to your massage to help relax tight muscles, improve circulation, and deepen relaxation.

### Aromatherapy

Essential oils added to your massage to enhance relaxation, reduce stress, and improve overall mood and wellbeing.

### Steam Eye Mask

A warm steam eye mask placed during your massage to help relieve eye strain, reduce stress, and enhance relaxation.

### Foot Scrub

A relaxing foot treatment that includes exfoliation, massage, and warm stones to soften skin, relieve tension, and improve circulation.
