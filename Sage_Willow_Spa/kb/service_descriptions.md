# Sage & Willow Spa — Service Descriptions

What each massage actually is, in plain language. Use these descriptions to help callers pick the right service. **Prices and durations come from the live get_services tool — do not quote prices from this document.** This is for descriptive answers only.

When a caller asks about a specific service, give the description in 1–2 sentences. If they're choosing between options, you can briefly compare two. Never read the full list as a menu.

---

## Signature Massage

The Signature Massage is our most popular option — a fully **customized treatment** that blends different massage techniques based on what your body needs that day. The therapist may mix Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone, and other modalities depending on whether you want to focus on relaxation, tension relief, or both. Available in three lengths.

**Best for:** clients who aren't sure which style they want, or who want the therapist to adapt to how they're feeling that day.

---

## Swedish Massage

A gentle, relaxing **full-body massage** using long, flowing strokes. It's designed to reduce stress, improve circulation, and promote overall relaxation. The pressure is light to medium — soothing rather than intense.

**Best for:** first-time massage clients, anyone who finds deeper pressure uncomfortable, or anyone who just wants to unwind.

---

## Deep Tissue Massage

A focused massage using **firm pressure** to target deeper layers of muscles. It helps relieve chronic tension, muscle tightness, and pain — especially in the back, shoulders, and neck. It can be intense at moments, but the therapist adjusts pressure to your comfort.

**Best for:** people with chronic muscle pain, athletes, or anyone with significant tension that lighter massage doesn't reach.

---

## Hot Stone Massage

A deeply relaxing massage that combines **heated stones** with traditional massage techniques. The warmth helps loosen tight muscles, improve circulation, and create a profoundly calming experience.

**Best for:** colder days, deep relaxation, or anyone who finds heat soothing.

---

## Prenatal Massage

A gentle, soothing massage **designed specifically for expecting mothers**. It uses safe positioning and light pressure to help relieve muscle tension, reduce stress, and improve comfort during pregnancy. We recommend checking with your doctor before booking, just to be safe.

**Best for:** pregnant clients in any trimester (with doctor's approval).

---

## Lymphatic Drainage Massage

A **gentle, light-pressure** massage that stimulates the lymphatic system to help reduce swelling, support detoxification, and promote overall wellness. It feels much lighter than a regular massage — the focus is on slow, rhythmic movements.

**Best for:** clients dealing with swelling, post-surgery recovery (with doctor's approval), or anyone interested in wellness-focused work rather than tension relief.

---

## Thirty-Minute Focus Massage

A short **thirty-minute** session that can either focus on a specific area you want worked on (back, shoulders, neck, legs, or feet), or serve as a quick full-body relaxation session.

**Best for:** people on a tight schedule, lunch breaks, or clients who want spot treatment for a specific tight area.

---

## Add-On Enhancements

Small extras that can be added to **any massage** to deepen the experience. Each one adds a small amount to the price — quote the live add-on price from the get_services tool when the caller asks.

### Hot Stone Enhancement

Heated stones added to your regular massage to help relax tight muscles, improve circulation, and deepen relaxation. A nice touch when you don't want a full Hot Stone Massage but enjoy the warmth.

### Aromatherapy

Essential oils added to your massage to enhance relaxation, reduce stress, and improve mood. The therapist will offer a couple of scents to choose from.

### Steam Eye Mask

A warm steam eye mask placed during your massage to help relieve eye strain, reduce stress, and enhance relaxation. Especially nice for anyone who spends long hours on screens.

### Foot Scrub

A relaxing foot treatment that includes exfoliation, massage, and warm stones. It softens skin, relieves tension, and improves circulation in the feet — a great addition if you've been on your feet a lot.

## Service Selection Guide (for Aria's reference)

If a caller is undecided, suggest based on what they say:

- **"I want to relax"** → Swedish or Hot Stone
- **"My back/shoulders/neck hurts"** → Deep Tissue
- **"I'm pregnant"** → Prenatal (with doctor's approval)
- **"I have swelling" or "post-surgery"** → Lymphatic Drainage (with doctor's approval)
- **"I'm short on time"** → Thirty-Minute Focus
- **"I'm not sure"** → Signature (let the therapist customize)
- **"I want extras"** → Mention available add-ons (Hot Stone, Aromatherapy, Steam Eye Mask, Foot Scrub)
